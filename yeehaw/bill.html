Bill Pickett
Willie M. "Bill" Pickett was a cowboy, rodeo, Wild West show performer and actor. In 1989, Pickett was inducted into the ProRodeo Hall of Fame.Bill Pickett was born in the Jenks-Branch community of Williamson County, Texas in 1870.Pickett left school in the 5th grade to become a ranch hand; he soon began to ride horses and watch the longhorn steers of his native Texas.He invented the technique of bulldogging, the skill of grabbing cattle by the horns and wrestling them to the ground.
